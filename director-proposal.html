02 · The Opportunity

A clear gap in the Singapore market

Singapore has over 280,000 SMEs, with a growing number of home-based and pick-up focused businesses that need digital ordering and presence tools — but cannot afford traditional web development.

The Problem

Traditional web development in Singapore costs SGD 15,000–50,000 for a custom ordering platform or web application. Turnaround times of 2–4 months are standard. For a home-based food business or small pick-up operation, this is completely out of reach.

The Solution

Operon delivers the same quality product at a fraction of the cost and time — using an intelligent, systems-driven build process. Clients get a production-ready platform with ongoing maintenance included from day one.

Primary Target Market
🏠 Home-Based Businesses

Singapore has seen significant growth in registered home-based food and retail businesses post-2020. These operators need professional ordering systems but lack the budget for traditional agencies.

🥡 Pick-Up Focused Operations

F&B businesses, specialty drink brands, and small retailers offering pick-up (not delivery) need streamlined ordering platforms — a niche Operon has already validated with Repeat Order.

06 · Operations Plan

How Operon runs day-to-day

Operon is designed to be lean from the start — a one-person operation in Year 1 with a structured plan to bring in talent as client volume grows.

👤
Leadership

Operon is led day-to-day by Fathul Iman (CEO, THEONEPERCENT HOLDINGS), who oversees all client delivery, product development, and business development in Year 1.

🛠️
Build Stack

All products are built on a proven, production-tested modern stack. React / Vite is the frontend framework used to build the user interface. GitHub serves as the source control and code repository — code is versioned here and Vercel auto-deploys from it. Supabase handles the backend, database, and authentication. Stripe processes payments. Vercel hosts and deploys the frontend. Resend delivers transactional emails such as order confirmations.

⚡
Delivery Speed

A standard client website or ordering platform is delivered within 1–2 weeks of project kickoff. The streamlined, systems-driven build process enables consistent, high-quality output at a pace traditional agencies cannot match.

📈
Team Expansion Plan

As client volume grows beyond 10 active accounts, Operon will evaluate bringing in a part-time developer or project coordinator. Expansion will be driven by revenue, not anticipated ahead of it.

🔒
Client Retention

The SGD 150/month retainer model creates a strong incentive for retention: clients who rely on Operon for ongoing maintenance have low churn motivation. Support is delivered remotely via messaging channels.
